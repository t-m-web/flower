Flower Bouquet By: Ellyse began as a love letter to Cebu — a small studio on Jones Avenue where Ellyse arranges every bouquet by hand. What started as gifts for friends quickly grew into a trusted neighborhood florist serving birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, and the moments in between.

Today we deliver across Cebu City daily, sourcing the freshest stems each morning and finishing every order with a personal note from Ellyse herself.
