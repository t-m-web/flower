How long does delivery take?
Within Cebu City, same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Outside the metro typically arrives next business day.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery, GCash, Bank Transfer, and PayPal — all available at checkout.
What is your refund policy?
If your bouquet arrives damaged or you're unhappy with it, contact us within 24 hours and we'll replace it or refund you in full.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes! Orders placed before 2:00 PM are delivered the same day within Cebu City.
Can I order a custom bouquet?
Absolutely. Message us on WhatsApp at +63 956 650 4470 with your colors, budget and occasion and Ellyse will design something just for you.
Do you deliver outside Cebu City?
We deliver to Mandaue, Lapu-Lapu, and Talisay daily. Other areas are next-day. Delivery fees vary by location.

Yes! Add your message at checkout under "Personalized Gift Message" — we'll handwrite it on a complimentary card.
